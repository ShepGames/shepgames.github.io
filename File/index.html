Passport To Independence






Welcome!

Welcome to the Passport to independence app! This app helps you track your progress, compete with your friends and gather points from doing so. Points can be used to unlock new characters and awards.

Your data

Your data is entirely stored using cookies. This means data is stored on your device's browser. Please do not clear cookies, otherwise your data will be lost. Use the same browser, as the cookies will not be present.

If you lose your data

If you do indeed lose your data, you can get it back with the help of an adult instructor.
