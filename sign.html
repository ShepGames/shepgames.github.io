Sign and magnitude

Binary can be used to represent negative numbers in many ways. One of those ways is sign and magnitude

Sign and magnitude encode integers so the number is read as either positive or negative.

A value at the left side of the binary number represents whether it is negative or positive.

00001001 is 9

10001001 is -9 using sign and magnitude

2s compliment

2s compliment is a method of representing negative numbers by making the negative numbers start with a 1.

2s compliment takes the normal binary number and inverses it, then adds 1.

0011 = 2

1100 + 0001 = 1101 which is -2

When using sign and magnitude in addition, always ignore the overflow number.
