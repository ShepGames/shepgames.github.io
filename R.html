R method

The R method is a way to simplify expressions.

4sinx+2cosx is difficult to solve. So we make it equal Rsin(x-α)

We can expand the formula using the double angle method: Rsinxcosα-Rsinαcosx

If you look at this equation, you can see that there is a sinx and a cosx, like in the other.

Lets take the 4sinx and Rsinxcosα

When the object is in uniform, we know the mass of the object is in the middle.

4 = Rcosα if we remove the sinx

2= -Rsinα

We then use simultaneous equations to find out both values.

Cosine

We can use the same method for the cosine! sin θ − b cos θ ≡ R cos α sin θ − R sin α cos θ

Max and Min

Sometimes we are asked to find the minimum and maximum of the graph Rsin(x-α)

It is mostly about understanding the wave of sin and cos. An easier way is to differentate and make it equal 0.

The values you get can be checked in the second derivative
