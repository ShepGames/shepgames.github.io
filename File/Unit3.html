Unit 3


Washing

Clearly demonstrate how to separate clothes for washing, understand the different washing machine programmes for different types of clothes and select them appropriately.
Assess how often clothes and linen should be washed, and understand the relevant detergents to do this.

Drying

Understand which items can be dried in a machine and what might happen if inappropriate items are dried.
Read labels to this effect and demonstrate ability to sort clothes.

Ironing

Demonstrate competence in ironing various items of laundry to include a shirt/blouse and trousers/skirt.
Understand the operation of an iron and the general principles involved.

Repairs

Change light bulbs
Understand how to make small repairs before replacing items.
Demonstrate the difference between need and want.
Demonstrate the need to budget for replacements.
