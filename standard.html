Standard deviation and stats

Mean

The mean is all your data added up and divided by the number of items: Σx/n

Quartile 1:

n/4

Quartile 2:

n/2

Quartile 3:

3n/4

Standard deviation

Standard deviation is the mean distance between points of data.

This is found by using the following formula:

√((Σx^2/n)-(Σx/n)^2)

Variance

This is simply the standard deviation sqquared

((Σx^2/n)-(Σx/n)^2)
