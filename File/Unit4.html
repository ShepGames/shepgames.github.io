Unit 4


Budgeting

Clearly understand personal income and expenditure.
Demonstrate where savings can be made in some areas
Understand priority on spending, savings or luxury items.
Understand the difference between need and want.
Have ID and a bank account.

Understand how to apply for and maintain a claim.
Understand the importance of contact with benefit agencies.
Understand the need to provide evidence to benefit agencies.
Understand sanctions and changes in benefit legislation
Understand the need for honesty with the DWP
Make and maintain a benefit claim/ maintain employment and manage wages.

Debts

Identify current debts, if any
Understand the importance of keeping out of debt wherever possible and ways to agree payments.
Demonstrate awareness of how debt can affect your future
Understand how and who to ask for help.

Credit

Explain how and why people use credit.
Be aware of the pros and cons of credit, and the different types of credit available.
Demonstrate how to work out the costs of credit.

Housing Costs

Be aware of the costs associated with moving into permanent accommodation.
Provide a clear plan for their move out costs and create an action plan for this.
Understand and demonstrate how to manage bills and plan for payment.
Understand how and why to minimise energy consumption.

Saving

Understand the value in saving and planning.
Understand saving options.
Understand the importance of a bank account.
Know where to seek advice on money problems.
Be aware of future savings options such as pensions and personal finances
