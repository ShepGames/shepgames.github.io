About this app

This app was designed by a BHASVIC student to help young people in the YMCA track their progress within their course.

How to use

There is a simple menu button next to the YMCA logo, at the top. This allows the user to navigate round the application. The passport page is where you will find your course. Simply select evidence using the evidence box. You can then show this to your instructor, where they can sign off your task.
