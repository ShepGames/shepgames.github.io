Unit 5


Cleaning

Understand the importance of keeping the home clean in relation to bacteria and hygienic practice.
Demonstrate how to clean their living space and which products to use to the best effect and maintain it.
Understand the risks of infestation and how to access help.

Appliance Care

Demonstrate and be aware of how to care for an appliance in order to prolong its useful life.
Understand and demonstrate health and safety practices.

Maintenance

Understand how to complete small tasks around the home.
Can evidence how to decorate within the home.
Demonstrate a good understanding of health and safety in regard to fire and infestation risk.
v

Security

Demonstrate an understanding of how to secure a home.
Understand how to manage visitors within their home.
Demonstrate how to ask for help in an emergency.
