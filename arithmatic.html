Modulus

Modulus division is where the we find the amount of times we can do the division of a number by another number.

8%2 is 0 because there is no remainder.

8%3 = 2 because 3+3 = 6, 3+3+3 = 9, which means it goes in twice. and has a remainder of 2. 10%3 = 1 as it has 3+3+3 and a remainder of 1
