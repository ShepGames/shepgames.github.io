Unit 2


Access to services

Be registered with a GP, Dentist, Optician and sexual health services.
Be aware of the importance of accessing all of these services.
Demonstrate understanding of how to find services in a new area.

Personal Hygiene

Understand the importance of personal hygiene, both physically and to self esteem.
Be aware of how personal hygiene affects other people and how to address issues such as body odour and skin complaints.
Demonstrate a positive self image through their appearance.

Health

Understand the value of good health.
Understand the importance of a balanced diet and exercise, and the impact this can have on physical health.
Understand who and how to ask for help
Understand the effects of substances on physical and mental health.
Understand how to use substances responsibly.

Ensuring services meet personal expectations

Be aware of the process of making complaints about services and who to contact to do so
Displays and understanding of entitlement to free or low cost services

Mental Wellbeing

Demonstrate an understanding of mental health issues.
Understand what mental health is.
Understand how lifestyle and choices can affect mental wellbeing.
Understand the effects of substances on mental health.
Establish and maintain positive relationships with family, friends, groups, cultural and faith groups.
